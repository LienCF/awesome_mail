Today’s the day. We’re officially launching Trolley as Topcoder’s new global payment platform.

 

Starting October 22, 2025, all Topcoder payments will now be processed through Trolley. Payoneer and PayPal are no longer supported for new payouts, but you can now receive your earnings via Trolley through Bank transfer (local or international). You’ll still have access to your existing funds in Payoneer or PayPal - they just won’t receive new payments from Topcoder after October 22.

 

Why Trolley?
We’ve made this change to give you a faster, more reliable, and more flexible payment experience. Trolley offers:

  • Lower overall fees (compared to Payoneer)

  • Support for 210+ countries and territories

  • Faster processing and better payout tracking

  • Bank-grade security (SOC 2 certified, GDPR compliant)

  • Easy account setup and tracking

  • Improved reporting and payout history

 

What you need to do:
Click the link below to securely set up your Trolley payout method in the Topcoder Wallet App: Set Up My Trolley Payment Method.

The setup only takes a few minutes.
