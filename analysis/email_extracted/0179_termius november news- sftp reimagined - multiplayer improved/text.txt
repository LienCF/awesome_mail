⚡️ Real-Time Collaboration, Now More Reliable Than Ever
-------------------------------------------------------

Multiplayer in Termius uses *WebRTC* to create a secure, end-to-end encrypted, peer-to-peer connection between teammates. However, until now, collaboration sessions have often failed to connect when participants were behind strict NAT or firewall configurations.

That's no longer the case.

We've added *several* *TURN* servers to handle those edge cases, ensuring your Multiplayer sessions connect smoothly and stay stable, regardless of the network setup.

Whether you're guiding a teammate through a tricky deployment or pair-debugging a production issue, you can now rely on a more resilient connection every time.

Read the full story behind the Multiplayer → ( https://termius.com/blog/from-chaos-to-clarity-reimagining-real-time-collaboration-in-the-terminal?utm_source=newsletter )

-----------------------------
📁 SFTP. Reimagined for Mobile
-----------------------------

We've completely reimagined SFTP in Termius to make file management on mobile faster, smoother, and more flexible than ever.

The classic two-pane layout was great for one-off transfers. Unfortunately, it made switching between servers and pushing files to multiple environments difficult. The new SFTP changes that.

You can now:

* Open *multiple SFTP* connections in tabs.
* *Switch between Terminal and SFTP* with a single tap.
* Track file transfers in real-time and view *progress at a glance*.
* Access and upload files directly from your device's storage.

Whether you're updating configs, reviewing logs, or moving assets between servers, Termius now makes it easy from your phone.

The new SFTP experience, launched on iOS earlier this year, is now rolling out on Android.

Read the full story → ( https://termius.com/blog/rethinking-sftp-for-mobile?utm_source=newsletter )
