$102 & up—Premium ferry rides: Seattle to Victoria
By Jeff Kirshman  |  Source: FRS Clipper

Why you should go: This direct ferry takes you from Seattle to Vancouver Island in less than three hours. Sit back and enjoy the ride between Pier 69 and Belleville Terminal in downtown Victoria. Along the way, keep an eye out for harbor seals and sea lions.

What's the big deal? The FRS Clipper offers the only direct fast-ferry service from Seattle. Monday–Thursday one-way trips out of Seattle or Victoria cost $102–$118 (reg. $124–$144). Roundtrip tickets are available for $127–$171 (reg. $153–$208).
