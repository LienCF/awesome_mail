EXPO 2025 OSAKA KANSAI Official Email Newsletter FINAL

*Expo 2025 Osaka, Kansai Japan came to a close yesterday, 13 October. It was a great success, with more than twenty million visitors. We would like to express our sincerest gratitude to everyone who visited and everyone who made the Expo possible.
This will also be the final issue of the email newsletter we have been sending out since last year. Thank you very much for reading it.
Other Expos will continue to be held after Expo 2025 Osaka, Kansai, Japan.
In 2027, Japan will host an International Horticultural Expo, and Serbia will host a Specialised Expo with the theme "Play for Humanity." Then in 2030, Expo 2030 Riyadh will be held in Saudi Arabia.
We look forward to seeing you again then!*

*The next Expo will be held in Yokohama.*

*Starting from March 19th, 2027, "GREEN×EXPO 2027 (International Horticultural Expo 2027)" will be held in Yokohama! Under the theme of "Scenery of the Future for Happiness," we will work with people around the world to create concrete actions toward a sustainable, green society in order to solve global issues such as climate change and degrading biodiversity. Visitors will be greeted by a spectacular sight of 10 million flowers and greenery and will also be able to experience food and culture from around the world. Our official mascot, " Tunku Tunku," will be waiting for you. We hope you will look forward to GREEN×EXPO 2027, which will carry on the excitement and inspiration of the Expo 2025 Osaka, Kansai, Japan. Please check out the information* *here* ( https://expo2027yokohama.or.jp/en/ ) *.*
