Dear future Sailor,

As we stood on Brilliant Lady's deck, sailing past Lady Liberty into New York Harbor, I couldn't help but think about you — those who've shown interest in what we're building but haven't yet stepped aboard.

You've been watching. You've been curious. And now, with our fourth kid-free ship joining the fleet, this brilliant moment feels like the perfect time to say: your adventure is ready when you are.

During our NYC arrival, 2,400 voices — passengers and crew — joined together to sing Queen's "Don't Stop Me Now" as Manhattan welcomed us home. We chose that song because we're just getting started. As our friend Astrid Jorgensen from Pub Choir said, "When we sing alone, we may not sound like a star, but when we sing together, it sounds brilliant."

That's exactly what Virgin Voyages is about: coming together, celebrating life, and creating moments that matter. Four magnificent Lady Ships where you can be brilliantly, authentically yourself, while connecting with like-minded people.

At a time when the world can sometimes feel less joyful, we're here to remind you that being with those we love — that's where joy lives. We have a simple philosophy at Virgin: be the reason someone smiles today.

This isn't the end of our story - it's the next brilliant chapter, and we're just getting started.

Ready to see what all the fuss is about?

With endless optimism,
Richard
