Just a quick reminder - we’re launching Trolley as Topcoder’s new global payment platform in one week!

 

Go-live: Tuesday, Oct 21, 2025, at 02:00 AM ET
Expected transition time: approximately 3 hours
During this window, payments will be temporarily unavailable while we complete the switch.

 

What’s Changing
•  All Topcoder payments will be processed through Trolley.

•  Payoneer and Paypal will no longer be supported for new payouts (You’ll still have access to your existing funds in Payoneer or PayPal — they just won’t receive new payments from Topcoder after October 21).

 

Note: Your Topcoder wallet balance will remain unchanged and you can still withdraw your payment after setting up Trolley.

 

What You’ll Be Able to Use
• Bank transfer (local or international)

 

What You Need to Do
• No action needed right now.

• On October 21, you’ll receive an email with instructions to set up your Trolley payout method in the Topcoder Wallet App. Setup only takes a few minutes.

 

Learn More
• Read the Trolley Overview article 

• Ask questions in the forums

 

We appreciate your patience during the short transition window and can’t wait to bring you a smoother, more reliable payment experience with Trolley.
