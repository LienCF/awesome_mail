A new unified Google AI Studio playground

Try the new AI playground

The new playground is a single, unified surface where you can use Gemini, GenMedia (with new Veo 3.1 capabilities), text-to-speech (TTS), and Live models, all without losing your place or switching tabs.

 

As part of this, we’ve also refined the entire chat UI for a more predictable and powerful interactive experience. Now you can go from prompt to image to video to TTS in one continuous flow.

A smarter start, clearer usage, and deeper context

We’ve also upgraded key parts of the platform for better visibility and creative power from the very beginning:

  • New welcome homepage: Explore your new command center to get you back into your projects instantly.
  • New rate limit page: Get a clear, real-time view of your usage and limits to better manage scale.
  • Maps grounding: Bring real-world geospatial data and context directly into your creative workflow.

More control over your prompts and keys

We shipped two of the most highly requested features to give you more granular control and save you time:

  • Saved system instructions: You no longer need to repeat yourself. Save, create templates, and reuse your instructions across chats, solving a major workflow pain point.
  • Revamped API key page: We’ve completely overhauled the API key page with project grouping and renaming, making it easier to manage your credentials.

This week was about building a better foundation. Next week, we’re going to build something magical on top of it. Get ready for Zero-to-Magic week, where we’ll introduce a new way for you to go from a single idea to a working, AI-powered app faster than we ever thought possible.
