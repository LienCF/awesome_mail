Yearly offsite

The whole team got together in Rovinj, Croatia, this year to celebrate our yearly offsite. Aside from all the travel disruptions, we had such a great time together!

One of the things I love the most about our offsites is that it’s mainly centered around people. Hanging out, doing fun activities, playing games, etc and not full of work meetings for a whole week! We did hold a work-related session in the morning, where Thomas and Petr shared an overview of how Raycast is doing and what we’ve got planned for the next year. And wow. I hope you’re ready!

I selfishly took this opportunity to take a bunch of photos which I always enjoy doing, you can check them out here to get a feel for the culture at Raycast. Thanks to Sandy for organising this event. It gets harder each year as the company grows.


A little glass

We’ve added a touch of Liquid Glass on both our macOS and iOS apps. Enjoy a refreshed look, featuring rounder corners, updated app icons, and smooth progressive blurs. Thomas shared some beautiful images of how it looks in Tahoe and iOS 26. We also made a little video where we talk about it a bit more. Check it out if you’re curious.
