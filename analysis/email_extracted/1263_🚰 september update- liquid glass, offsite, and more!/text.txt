YEARLY OFFSITE

The whole team got together in Rovinj, Croatia, this year to celebrate our
yearly offsite. Aside from all the travel disruptions, we had such a great time
together!

One of the things I love the most about our offsites is that it’s mainly
centered around people. Hanging out, doing fun activities, playing games, etc
and not full of work meetings for a whole week! We did hold a work-related
session in the morning, where Thomas and Petr shared an overview of how Raycast
is doing and what we’ve got planned for the next year. And wow. I hope you’re
ready!

I selfishly took this opportunity to take a bunch of photos which I always enjoy
doing, you can check them out here
https://x.com/peduarte/status/1973389535657472149 to get a feel for the culture
at Raycast. Thanks to Sandy for organising this event. It gets harder each year
as the company grows.





A LITTLE GLASS

We’ve added a touch of Liquid Glass on both our macOS and iOS apps. Enjoy a
refreshed look, featuring rounder corners, updated app icons, and smooth
progressive blurs. Thomas shared some beautiful images of how it looks in Tahoe
https://x.com/thomaspaulmann/status/1967920772896395680 and iOS 26
https://x.com/thomaspaulmann/status/1968283679442239813. We also made a little
video where we talk about it a bit more. Check it out
https://x.com/peduarte/status/1967903168995754451 if you’re curious.





FUTURE FIFTY 2025

This year, we got selected to be part of the Future Fifty 2025 by TechNation.
Future Fifty is where late-stage UK tech levels up, with 30%+ of the country’s
unicorns coming through the program. We’re thrilled to join the lineup. Learn
more about it here https://technation.io/programmes/future-fifty/.
