Accenture is federating their identity provider SSO to NVIDIA NGC.Hello Org owner(s), <br>
<br>
NVIDIA is federating Accenture's Identity Provider (IdP) service to enhance access control for NVIDIA NGC. Your current NVIDIA identity (NVIDIA Accounts) will be replaced by your corporate Accenture SSO credentials. This means your NVIDIA user identity will be replaced by an identity generated by your corporate Accenture SSO service, and we will perform an account transfer when your company's IdP becomes active. <br>
<br>
Your NGC access will be temporarily interrupted when your company's IdP service is enabled. We appreciate your cooperation in minimizing this interruption. <br>
<br>
Please send your email address and NGC org name (found under "Organization Profile" settings) before October 13th, 2025, to ngc-sso@nvidia.com. <br>
<br>
Thank you,<br>
The NVIDIA NGC Team.
