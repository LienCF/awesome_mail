We’re changing how to earn Aeroplan points with Air Canada and achieve Aeroplan Elite status
 
 
 
For travel on or after January 1, 2026, earn Aeroplan points based on how much is spent on eligible flights with Air Canada® rather than the distance flown. Learn more
 
 
 
 
Also, effective January 1, 2026, we’re changing how to qualify for Aeroplan Elite® status in 2027 and beyond. Learn more
 
 
 

Here’s how to earn Aeroplan points with Air Canada
 
These updates will apply for travel starting on or after January 1, 2026.
 
 
 

Earn 1pt/$1 spent on Air Canada flights*
Earn points based on how much is spent on eligible Air Canada flights.

*Points are earned based on the base fare and carrier surcharges, excluding applicable taxes, fees and third‑party charges on eligible Air Canada flights.
 
 
 

More ways to earn points
Members will benefit from a broader range of bonus point offers.
 
 
 

Earn on Air Canada Vacations® packages
Earn a fixed amount of Aeroplan points based on cabin selection and destination.
 
 
 
 

Boost earnings with Aeroplan Elite status
Elite members will earn between 2X and 6X the Aeroplan points on eligible Air Canada flights, depending on their Status at the time of travel.

Here’s how to qualify for Aeroplan Elite status in 2027 and beyond – plus, introducing SQC Milestones
 
On January 1, 2026, we’re introducing Status Qualifying Credits (SQC)‡—the new way to qualify for Aeroplan Elite status.

With these changes, SQC with Air Canada will be based on how much is spent:

  • Get 4 SQC per dollar spent± on eligible fares that are Economy Flex or higher

  • Get 2 SQC per dollar spent± on eligible Economy Standard fares and eUpgrade add‑ons


Members can also accumulate up to 25,000 SQC with our Aeroplan partners and up to 25,000 SQC with an Aeroplan credit card.
 
 
 
 

Introducing SQC Milestones
For every 10,000 SQC accumulated, Milestones will be unlocked. Some will award benefits automatically, while others will offer a choice to members. Milestone benefits include bonus SQC, Priority Rewards, Status Passes and more.

We’re listening—and making meaningful updates
 
Your feedback continues to shape the Aeroplan Elite experience. Since announcing these updates, we've made further refinements based on what you've shared with us:

  • Inclusion of Star Alliance flights ticketed by Air Canada:
     The  Elite Points Multiplier will apply to Star Alliance flights ticketed by Air Canada (014 tickets). Learn more

  • Extension of 2025 Status for a Friend: If you requalify for your Aeroplan 75K or Super Elite status for 2026, your nominated friend’s status will automatically be extended through 2026. This ensures a smooth transition to the Milestone Benefits in 2026. Learn more

  • Million Mile updates: Lifetime Qualifying Miles (LQM) will maintain a cabin bonus for Premium Economy and Business Class fares, plus new annual benefits will be introduced for Million Mile members. Learn more
