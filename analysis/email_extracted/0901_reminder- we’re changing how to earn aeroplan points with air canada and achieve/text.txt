HERE’S HOW TO QUALIFY FOR AEROPLAN ELITE STATUS IN 2027 AND BEYOND – PLUS,
INTRODUCING SQC MILESTONES

 

On January 1, 2026, we’re introducing Status Qualifying Credits (SQC)‡—the new
way to qualify for Aeroplan Elite status.

With these changes, SQC with Air Canada will be based on how much is spent:

 * Get 4 SQC per dollar spent± on eligible fares that are Economy Flex or higher

 * Get 2 SQC per dollar spent± on eligible Economy Standard fares and eUpgrade
   add‑ons


Members can also accumulate up to 25,000 SQC with our Aeroplan partners and up
to 25,000 SQC with an Aeroplan credit card.

INTRODUCING SQC MILESTONES

For every 10,000 SQC accumulated, Milestones will be unlocked. Some will award
benefits automatically, while others will offer a choice to members. Milestone
benefits include bonus SQC, Priority Rewards, Status Passes and more.

WE’RE LISTENING—AND MAKING MEANINGFUL UPDATES

 

Your feedback continues to shape the Aeroplan Elite experience. Since announcing
these updates, we've made further refinements based on what you've shared
with us:

 * Inclusion of Star Alliance flights ticketed by Air Canada:
   The  Elite Points Multiplier will apply to Star Alliance flights ticketed by
   Air Canada (014 tickets). Learn more
   [https://t.mail.aircanada.com/r/?id=hbc132024,22301f24,10dd9be8]

 * Extension of 2025 Status for a Friend: If you requalify for your Aeroplan 75K
   or Super Elite status for 2026, your nominated friend’s status will
   automatically be extended through 2026. This ensures a smooth transition to
   the Milestone Benefits in 2026. Learn more
   [https://t.mail.aircanada.com/r/?id=hbc132024,22301f24,10dd9be9]

 * Million Mile updates: Lifetime Qualifying Miles (LQM) will maintain a cabin
   bonus for Premium Economy and Business Class fares, plus new annual benefits
   will be introduced for Million Mile members. Learn more
   [https://t.mail.aircanada.com/r/?id=hbc132024,22301f24,10dd9beb]
