What's new on PostHog this month?

 

Max AI explores strange, new formats

You can now get monthly changelog highlights in ultra-nerdy video format over on our YouTube page. This month the crew of Starship Changelog explore Max AI's new abilities in experiments, surveys, and analytics. HD video. Big crunchy pixels.

Watch, like, and subscribe
 

Messaging Workflows now in feature preview

We've widened the scope of our messaging product to include a lot more than just emails, and renamed it as workflows. But you can also still send messages with it! Join the preview to start building event-triggered email campaigns in PostHog. 

Join the workflows preview
 

It's now cheaper to send data elsewhere

TL;DR: We now charge for data pipelines based on rows exported and trigger events. It matches our pricing principles better and means 98% of you will now pay less. You'll also benefit from much bigger free tiers. So, please use data pipelines more to help us maintain our revenue? The shareholders would appreciate it. 

Explore 145 possible integrations
 

Meta Ads now available as a data source

We've added Meta Ads as a new data source in PostHog CDP, enabling you to pull campaign data and performance metrics like clicks and impressions into PostHog. This sort of data is especially useful for our new Marketing Analytics beta too!

Join the marketing analytics beta
 

Now available: Unlimited tabs, easier search

Multi-taskers, rejoice! You can now open multiple tabs within PostHog, giving you a much better way to organize your work. You can even set the new tab view, with its plentiful shortcuts and Max AI search bar, as your default. It's very Web 2.0, tbh.
