What this means
When service is paused, you can still use Wi‑Fi but won't have cell service to:
  • Receive or make calls
  • Receive or send text messages
  • Use cellular data
Financing or subscriptions
Monthly charges will continue for any phone payments or subscriptions you may have. Pausing Fi service doesn't interrupt any bundled subscription services.
Resuming service
While service will resume automatically on Jan 19, you can always resume it earlier. Whenever you resume service, you'll be charged for the remainder of that cycle. No plan charges apply if service is paused for the entire cycle.
