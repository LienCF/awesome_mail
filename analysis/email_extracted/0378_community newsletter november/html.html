Informix Migration: A Big Step Forward 

A few days ago, we reached a major milestone — saying goodbye to Informix after 24 years and moving most of the Topcoder platform to our new Postgres-powered system. This migration is one of the biggest upgrades in Topcoder history, built through 100+ community challenges that redesigned our databases, APIs, UIs, and more.

 

✅ Faster - Many pages and features are already loading quicker.
✅ Simpler - One modern database means a cleaner, more consistent platform. 

✅ Better for Everyone - from competitors and reviewers to copilots and admins.

✅ New Review App - a fresh experience with drag-and-drop uploads, Markdown support, and a modern interface.Check out more details.

 

We know some items still need to be migrated and a few rough edges remain - and we appreciate everyone who’s shared feedback to help us fine-tune the experience.


This isn’t the finish line, but it’s a huge leap forward toward a faster, more reliable, and community-built Topcoder.

Payment Transition: Welcome to Trolley

We’ve officially completed the transition from Payoneer and PayPal to Trolley - our new global payment provider.

With Trolley, you can now enjoy:
✅ Bank transfers to 210+ countries 

✅ Simplified tax forms and payout tracking

✅ Improved reporting and compliance

We know some members have experienced longer withdrawal times during the first few days of this rollout, and we appreciate your patience as Trolley fine-tunes the process. Our teams are working closely together to ensure smoother, faster payments going forward.

      Coming Soon: NASA Crater Detection Marathon Match 

      Ready to help NASA advance lunar exploration? 🚀

       

      In our exciting new Marathon Match, launching November 14, 2025, you’ll develop algorithms to detect crater rims in lunar imagery - a key step toward improving planetary navigation and future space travel.

       

      Your mission:
      ✅ Detect crater rims in lunar images with challenging lighting

      ✅ Fit ellipses to crater boundaries

      ✅ Help NASA improve optical navigation systems for lunar missions

       

      This is your chance to contribute to real NASA research, working on data that could help shape the future of space exploration.
