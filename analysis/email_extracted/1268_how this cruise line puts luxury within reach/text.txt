Sometimes you want to splash out on a cruise and splurge on everything. The
flights. The suite. The excursions. And that level of luxury often carries a big
price tag. But there is a way to avoid the sticker shock and still elevate your
experience.

On Emerald Cruises, the baseline for their river and yacht cruises is already
pretty great. Gourmet dining, expert-led cultural excursions and attentive
service are baked into the cruise price. But you can also take it up a notch
quite easily. Think: an indulgent spa day, truffle tastings in Croatia or
touring picturesque French vineyards. 

Discover what makes sailing with Emerald Cruises such an accessible luxury
experience.
