Hello!

We are writing to let you know that the **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** model will be **deprecated in Windsurf on October 22, 2025**. After this date, the model will no longer be available for use in the platform.

This change follows Anthropic’s official deprecation timeline for Claude 3.5 Sonnet ([see announcement here](https://docs.claude.com/en/docs/about-claude/model-deprecations#2025-08-13%3A-claude-sonnet-3-5-models)).

**What this means for you**:

 


- Any chats with Cascade or inline Commands using **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** will stop working after **October 22, 2025**. 
- We recommend migrating to **Sonnet 4.5** or one of the other Claude models currently available in Windsurf for continued access to Anthropic’s most capable offerings. 
We appreciate your understanding and thank you for continuing to build with Windsurf.
