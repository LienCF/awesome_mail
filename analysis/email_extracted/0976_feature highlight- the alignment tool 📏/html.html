Feature highlight: The alignment tool 📏

We know working on a canvas can be a stream-of-consciousness kind of thing. You take notes, write down ideas and create new documents. But you still want to keep it neat and organised.

> This is when the alignment tool is really useful.

It can look a bit intimidating with its many symbols, but don’t worry. In this email, we will cover all of the alignment options, and what they do.

🥇 Top tip

Let's start with something that shows what knowing the alignment tools allows you to do.

You get the best out of the tools when you combine them. E.g. the horizontal distribution and top alignment:



How the different alignments work

Now, let's cover each alignment tool separately.

👈 Align left
Aligns the left side of the selected items to the leftmost item.


👉Align right
Aligns the right side of the selected items to the rightmost item.


☝️ Align top
Aligns the top of the selected items to the highest placed item.


🫵 Center horizontally
The selected items meet in the middle around a common center.



↔️ Distribute horizontally
Distributes the selected items with equal distance from each other. All items will be evenly distributed horizontally, following the leftmost item.


❓What about vertical distribution?
The best way to vertically align elements is to stack them on top of each other. This way, the layout will not break even as you keep adding text.


We hope you’ve found this short guide helpful, and that you try it out yourself today if you haven't already!
