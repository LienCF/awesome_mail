The new Affinity has landed. It’s a completely reimagined, customizable desktop app that brings photo editing, vector design, and page layout together in one seamless space – fast, focused, and built for a pro creative workflow.

This first release is for Mac and Windows, with an iPad version coming soon. Until then, your Affinity V2 iPad apps will keep working as usual.

But if you’re curious, there’s no need to wait. You can download the new desktop app today. Like we said, it’s completely free. Because we believe creative freedom shouldn’t come with a price tag.
