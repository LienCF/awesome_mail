Hey there, it’s time to talk.

We’ve always built Anytype for people to create and connect on their own terms. It started with Spaces – a home for your thoughts, notes and projects.

With Chats, we’re opening a new chapter: a new way to collaborate while keeping your data fully in your hands.
Version 0.40
Chat on Mobile
Messages, replies, reactions – the basics are here. But this is Anytype: you can also add and edit objects right inside your chats. Capture thoughts as Tasks or Pages, share what you’re building, and co-create in real time. Everything stays organized in your widgets.
Notifications are here to keep you updated when it matters.
Invites work without approval – share a link or QR code and start chatting instantly.
