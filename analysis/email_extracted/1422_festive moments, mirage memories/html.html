Festive Moments, Mirage Memories

Celebrate the holidays in style at Centara Mirage Beach Resort Dubai! From sparkling tree lightings and whimsical Gingerbread House displays to dazzling Christmas shows, festive treats, and cosy mulled wine evenings, there’s joy around every corner. Unwind with curated spa treatments, share laughter with loved ones, and enjoy celebrations the entire family will never forget on Dubai Islands’ shimmering waterfront!

Lights of Wonder

Kick off the season with magical moments for all ages! Enjoy our dazzling Christmas Tree Lighting, admire the grand Gingerbread House display, delight in the lively Cracking Christmas Shows, and savour festive Sugar & Spice High Tea or warm mulled wine under twinkling lights.

 

The Eve of Magic

Celebrate Christmas Eve with delightful holiday menus at our signature restaurants. From traditional favourites with Asian or Arabian touches to live BBQs and à la carte specials, every dish is crafted to delight the senses—perfect for a joyful evening with family and friends.

 

Christmas Day Magic

Meet Santa and his elves at a special parade before indulging in an exceptional Christmas lunch! From hearty buffets and à la carte specialties to a festive BBQ, enjoy a day full of vibrant flavours and family fun. Continue the celebrations with a traditional Christmas lunch also served on 7th January.

 

Holiday Glow Awaits

Wrap up the year with pampering at Spa Cenvaree and our kid-focused Candy Spa. Treat yourself and loved ones to indulgent seasonal hampers, revitalising massages, and special holiday treatments. Bring joy, wellness, and renewal to the season with restorative experiences for all ages.

 

New Year's Eve Under the Stars

Welcome the New Year with an unforgettable beachfront celebration! Enjoy live music, DJ beats, saxophone melodies, and mesmerising belly dancers while indulging in a decadent feast under the stars. Celebrate the arrival of 2026 with family and friends on Dubai Islands’ shimmering shores.
