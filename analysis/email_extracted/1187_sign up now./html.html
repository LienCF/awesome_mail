Create immersive media experiences for visionOS

IETF HLS Interest Day

Join us for an all-day activity designed for engineers and professionals working with HTTP Live Streaming (HLS) technologies, such as video encoding, transcoding, CDNs, and playback applications. Press Start: Game development on Apple platforms

Unlock the full potential of game development for iOS, iPadOS, macOS, and visionOS. Whether you’re porting a PC or console title, crafting a mobile masterpiece, or augmenting your game with cutting-edge features, this full day of in-depth sessions will help you find success in the Apple ecosystem. You’ll learn how to leverage Metal, optimize for Apple silicon, design compelling handheld experiences, and navigate the App Store to reach millions of players.

STREAM ON DEMAND

Code along with the
Foundation Models framework

Recorded live in Cupertino: Discover how to use the Foundation Models framework to access Apple’s on-device LLM.
