[Oceanic+ App Update]

Dear Oceanic+ User,
 
We are excited to announce version 3.0.8 for Oceanic+ is now available for [download in the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/scuba-diving-computer-oceanic/id1610517133)! This update is part of our ongoing commitment to improving the App’s performance and reliability, driven by the valuable feedback we receive from our users.
 
Version 3.0.8 introduces several key improvements to deliver an even smoother and more reliable experience:

- Now fully compatible with the new iPhone 17 series — including iPhone 17, 17 Pro, 17 Pro Max, and 17 Air
- Optimized for iOS 26.1, ensuring seamless performance on all the latest devices
- Enhanced camera integration to support the new multi-lens and digital zoom systems introduced with iPhone 17 models
- Improved aspect-ratio handling (4:3, 1:1, and 16:9) for more consistent photo saving and better stability
- General performance enhancements for greater reliability underwater and during post-dive media review
Thank you for your feedback and support and keep exploring with Oceanic+!

[UPDATE NOW](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/scuba-diving-computer-oceanic/id1610517133)

 
As a reminder, iOS 18.0.1 and watchOS 18.0.1 are the minimum supported operating systems for Oceanic+ to operate. This limits certain iOS devices from operating the app. For a list of the compatible devices and iOS versions, [click here](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/iphone-models-compatible-with-ios-18-iphe3fa5df43/18.0/ios/18.0). To know which OS software version you are running on your iPhone or Watch, go to Settings, click on your name, then click on your device being used to see the information in 'Version'.
 
If you have any questions, please reach our team by replying to this email.
