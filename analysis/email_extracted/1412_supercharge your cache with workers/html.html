You're already using our CDN to bring your content closer to your users. To take your performance to the next level, have you considered using Workers for advanced cache customization? 


While our standard caching is powerful, Workers gives you granular control to:

  • Cache dynamic content: Serve personalized content from the edge.

  • Implement custom logic: Modify headers, A/B test, or reroute requests before they hit your origin.

  • Improve your cache hit ratio: Define exactly what gets cached and for how long, further reducing origin traffic and costs.

For any site, a small tweak in cache strategy can have a huge impact on performance and your origin server load.
