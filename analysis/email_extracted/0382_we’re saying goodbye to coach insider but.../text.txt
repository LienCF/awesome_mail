 Dear Insider,



As we form new ways to connect with the Coach community, we wanted to let you know that the Coach Insider program will end on November 6, 2025.



We remain committed to delivering exceptional customer service both in-store and online, offering distinctive shopping experiences and providing the high-quality products you know and love.



While this chapter is coming to a close, know that you will always be a valued part of the Coach community. No action is required on your part. Here are the details about this change:


Here’s what’s staying:

	- You can still 
https://www.coach.com/account-login 
log in  to your Coach account to view your purchase history, manage wishlists, enjoy a faster checkout and redeem (Re)Loved Exchange credits.

	- If you have existing (Re)Loved Exchange credits, they’ll remain active for one year from the date issued, just as always. You can redeem them online by logging in to your account or in store by sharing your account email with a sales associate.

	- The (Re)Loved Exchange program is here to stay—so you can continue to exchange used Coach bags for store credit. Learn more about (Re)Loved Exchange 
https://www.coach.com/support/coach-reloved 
here .
 

	- You can continue to enjoy the convenience of Easy Returns, now with even more flexibility. Starting today, all customers can return Coach and Coach Outlet items at any Coach or Coach Outlet store—no matter where they were purchased—as long as the items meet the terms of our return policy. 
Here’s what’s changing:

	- If you have unused Free Customization rewards (up to $125), be sure to redeem them by January 5, 2026. (Please note: free customization is only available for eligible Coach products; Coach Outlet products are excluded.)

	- Free shipping on all Coach and Coach Outlet orders will be available to existing Insiders until January 5, 2026. After that date, it will be available on orders of $75+.
