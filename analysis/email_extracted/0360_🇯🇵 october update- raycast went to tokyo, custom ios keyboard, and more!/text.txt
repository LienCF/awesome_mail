RAYCAST × TOKYO

We travelled to Tokyo to hang out with our community. Wow. Still trying to find the right words to express how beautiful it was. Japan is our third largest user base and for a while they've already been hosting some community-driven events. So we decided it was time for us to fly over there and connect with them in real life.

To make the trip worthwhile, we hosted two events.

 * Hackathon: We partnered with Anthropic https://www.anthropic.com/, Supabase https://supabase.com/, Vercel https://vercel.com/, and AWS Startup Loft https://aws.amazon.com/startups/lp/aws-startup-loft-tokyo to host a hackathon with amazing prizes and even better vibes. There were over 150 participants, it was crazy. They built Raycast extensions we never would've thought of.

 * Meetup: We also hosted a Raycast community meetup at the Cybozu https://cybozu.co.jp/ office. Around 60 people showed up. It was great. Thomas shared some sneak peeks of Raycast for Windows and some refreshed UI updates. The community did a few lightning talks, and Thomas, Steph and I held a fireside panel where people could ask us questions.

These events wouldn't have been possible without the help and collaboration of lots of people, so here's my heartfelt thanks to all of them: Our Japan Ambassadors and community members Yano https://twitter.com/@nagauta_jp, Tyki https://twitter.com/@tyykid16, Koyasu https://twitter.com/@ux_kys, Seiji https://x.com/se_eiji, Kaita https://twitter.com/@z63d_, Yogo https://twitter.com/@yogo_escentier, Adake https://x.com/yuichi_yogo, Hirota https://twitter.com/@HiroYuske, Tyler https://x.com/dshukertjr from Supabase for being a spectatucular MC and huge contributor to the hackathon, Jo https://x.com/jozhukennedy from Anthropic, Nicolas and Shohei https://x.com/jrsyo from Vercel, Kazuhiro https://twitter.com/@0b1tk and Sakito https://twitter.com/@sakito from Cybozu for hosting the meetup, AWS Startup Loft for hosting the hackathon, our Taiwan Amabassadors Yen https://x.com/ridemountainpig and Yiwei https://x.com/1weiho for fying over to just meet us, and finally Stephanie, our Community Manager for organising all. Thank you all so much!





KEYBOARD FOR IOS

Our custom keyboard puts dictation, AI commands, snippets, and quicklinks at your fingertips. This completely changes the Raycast experience on mobile. Building a keyboard was already in our roadmap when we initially announced Raycast for iOS, but it was a challenging feature to get right. Since launching the keyboard, we've received so much praise from our users!

Check out the video https://youtu.be/lta7_MCTzxo to see it in action!
